The Best Pizza Ever!

    Recipe:

  • Mix sugar, yeast and water in a bowl then let it sit for 5 minutes.
  • In another bowl, sift the flour and salt, then add the oil and the yeast mixture.
  • Mix until it has formed a dough, then knead for 4-5 minutes.
  • Put the dough in a bowl and cover with a damp cloth, put it in a warm place, and let it sit for 1-24 hours.
  • Preheat the oven to 450°F.
  • Using a floured hand, pinch the dough to knock the air out of it.
  • Divide the dough into 2, then roll out the dough.
  • Spread the pizza sauce on the rolled out dought evenly then sprinkle the cheese on top.
  • Top with the toppings of your choice.
  • Bake for 10-15 minutes until golden brown.
  • Broil for 1 minute for best results.
  • Enjoy!

    Ingredients:

  • Pasta Sauce (Amount Varies)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups lukewarm water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • Mozzarella Cheese (Amount Varies)
  • Toppings of your choice
